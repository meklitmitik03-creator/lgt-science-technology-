Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Description: AI is the field of computer science that focuses on creating machines capable of performing tasks that usually require human intelligence, such as learning, problem-solving, and decision-making.

Universities: Top universities offering AI programs include MIT, Stanford, Harvard, ETH Zurich, and Addis Ababa University.

Career Opportunities: AI engineer, data scientist, machine learning engineer, robotics programmer, research scientist.

Fun Fact: AI is being used in healthcare to predict diseases, in self-driving cars, and even in creating music and art!
