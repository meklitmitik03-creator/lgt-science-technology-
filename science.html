Biology

Study of living organisms and life processes.

Education: High school biology, advanced biology courses.

Universities: Addis Ababa University, Hawassa University.

Careers: Biologist, Doctor, Geneticist, Ecologist.

Fun Fact: The human body has over 37 trillion cells.

Chemistry

Study of substances, reactions, and properties of matter.

Education: High school chemistry, lab practice.

Universities: Jimma University, Mekelle University.

Careers: Chemist, Pharmacist, Lab Technician.

Fun Fact: Water can exist in three states at the same time under special conditions.

Physics

Study of matter, energy, and the universe.

Education: Physics courses, calculus, experiments.

Universities: Addis Ababa University, Bahir Dar University.

Careers: Physicist, Engineer, Researcher, Astronomer.

Fun Fact: Light from the Sun takes about 8 minutes to reach Earth.

Earth Science

Study of Earth's structure, land, water, and atmosphere.

Education: Geography, geology, environmental science courses.

Universities: Addis Ababa University, Jimma University.

Careers: Geologist, Meteorologist, Environmental Scientist.

Fun Fact: Volcanoes and earthquakes are part of Earth science.
