üåç Natural Science Branches

Biology

Study of living organisms and life processes.

Education: High school biology, advanced biology courses.

Universities: Addis Ababa University, Hawassa University.

Careers: Biologist, Doctor, Geneticist, Ecologist.

Fun Fact: The human body has over 37 trillion cells.

Chemistry

Study of substances, reactions, and properties of matter.

Education: High school chemistry, lab practice.

Universities: Jimma University, Mekelle University.

Careers: Chemist, Pharmacist, Lab Technician.

Fun Fact: Water can exist in three states at the same time under special conditions.

Physics

Study of matter, energy, and the universe.

Education: Physics courses, calculus, experiments.

Universities: Addis Ababa University, Bahir Dar University.

Careers: Physicist, Engineer, Researcher, Astronomer.

Fun Fact: Light from the Sun takes about 8 minutes to reach Earth.

Earth Science

Study of Earth's structure, land, water, and atmosphere.

Education: Geography, geology, environmental science courses.

Universities: Addis Ababa University, Jimma University.

Careers: Geologist, Meteorologist, Environmental Scientist.

Fun Fact: Volcanoes and earthquakes are part of Earth science.

üåê Social Science Branches

History

Study of past events, civilizations, and cultures.

Education: History courses, literature, cultural studies.

Universities: Addis Ababa University, Bahir Dar University.

Careers: Historian, Archaeologist, Teacher, Museum Curator.

Fun Fact: The oldest known written story is over 4,000 years old.

Economics

Study of resources, markets, and wealth distribution.

Education: Economics courses, math, statistics.

Universities: Addis Ababa University, Jimma University.

Careers: Economist, Financial Analyst, Policy Advisor, Banker.

Fun Fact: The concept of supply and demand dates back to the 18th century.

Sociology

Study of human society, behavior, and social relationships.

Education: Sociology, psychology, research methods.

Universities: Addis Ababa University, Mekelle University.

Careers: Sociologist, Social Worker, Researcher, Policy Analyst.

Fun Fact: Sociology helps explain why people behave differently in groups.

Political Science

Study of government, politics, and international relations.

Education: Political theory, history, law, international relations.

Universities: Addis Ababa University, Hawassa University.

Careers: Political Analyst, Diplomat, Government Officer, Lawyer.

Fun Fact: Political science helps predict election outcomes and policy impacts.
