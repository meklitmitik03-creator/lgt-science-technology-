Natural Science in Ethiopian Curriculum

Students study Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Earth Science.

Physics

Education: High school physics, lab experiments

Universities: Addis Ababa University, Bahir Dar University

Careers: Physicist, Engineer, Researcher

Scholarships & Countries: Erasmus, DAAD, USA, Germany

Chemistry

Education: High school chemistry, lab practice

Universities: Jimma University, Mekelle University

Careers: Chemist, Pharmacist, Lab Technician

Scholarships & Countries: Erasmus, Australia, Canada

Biology

Education: High school biology, advanced biology

Universities: Addis Ababa University, Hawassa University

Careers: Doctor, Biologist, Geneticist

Scholarships & Countries: USA, UK, Erasmus

Earth Science

Education: Geography, geology, environmental science courses

Universities: Addis Ababa University, Jimma University

Careers: Geologist, Meteorologist, Environmental Scientist

Scholarships & Countries: Germany, USA, Erasmus
