Social Science in Ethiopian Curriculum

Students study History, Economics, Sociology, and Political Science.

History

Study of past events

Education: High school history, literature, cultural studies

Universities: Addis Ababa University, Bahir Dar University

Careers: Historian, Archaeologist, Teacher

Scholarships & Countries: Erasmus, USA, UK

Economics

Study of markets and wealth

Education: Economics, math, statistics

Universities: Addis Ababa University, Jimma University

Careers: Economist, Financial Analyst, Policy Advisor

Scholarships & Countries: Erasmus, Germany, Canada

Sociology

Study of society

Education: Sociology, psychology, research methods

Universities: Addis Ababa University, Mekelle University

Careers: Sociologist, Social Worker, Researcher

Scholarships & Countries: USA, UK, Erasmus

Political Science

Study of government and politics

Education: Political theory, history, law, international relations

Universities: Addis Ababa University, Hawassa University

Careers: Political Analyst, Diplomat, Government Officer

Scholarships & Countries: USA, Germany, Erasmus
