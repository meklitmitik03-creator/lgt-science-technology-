About LGT

Welcome to LGT — a digital platform dedicated to celebrating Ethiopia’s growing achievements in science, technology, and innovation. Our mission is to inspire young learners and future inventors to build a brighter, smarter nation through knowledge and creativity.

Our Projects

Solar Energy Solutions: Creating low-cost solar devices to bring power to rural areas.

Smart Farming: Using sensors to help farmers monitor soil and rainfall for better harvests.

STEM Education: Encouraging students to learn coding, robotics, and green technology.

Innovation & Research

Ethiopia is entering a new era of digital transformation. From renewable energy to biotechnology and digital startups, innovation is changing how we live, learn, and work. LGT highlights these advancements and the young minds behind them.
