Welcome to the World of Science

Science connects ideas, discoveries, and technology to make our world a better place. Explore the difference between natural and social sciences below.

Natural Science

Natural science focuses on studying the physical world — including physics, chemistry, biology, astronomy, and geology. It explains how natural phenomena work and uses experiments to prove ideas.

Social Science

Social science studies human behavior, culture, and society — including geography, history, economics, and sociology. It helps us understand how people live, interact, and build civilizations.
