Welcome to the World of Science

Science is the study of nature, the universe, and how everything works together — from atoms to galaxies.

Natural Science

Natural science studies the physical world — physics, chemistry, biology, astronomy, and earth science. It seeks to understand how nature works through experiments and observation.

Social Science

Social science focuses on human behavior and society — including economics, history, geography, and sociology. It helps us understand how people interact and organize their lives.
